Recipe Information

This is a simple and delicious cake recipe that anyone can make at home. Perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or just a sweet treat!

Follow the steps below to create a delightful cake that will impress your family and friends.

Make sure to gather all the ingredients before you start baking.

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Cooking time: 35 minutes

Servings: 8

Difficulty: Easy

Enjoy your delicious homemade cake!

Happy baking!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Ingredient Photo

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, and sugar.
  3. Add the butter and mix until crumbly.
  4. In another bowl, beat the eggs and add milk and vanilla extract.
  5. Combine both mixtures and pour into a greased cake pan.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

Step Illustration

Tips

For a richer flavor, you can add chocolate chips or nuts to the batter. Make sure to let the cake cool completely before frosting it. You can use your favorite frosting recipe or store-bought frosting for convenience.

Notes

  • Store the cake in an airtight container to keep it fresh.
  • This recipe can be easily doubled for larger gatherings.
  • Feel free to experiment with different flavors by adding fruits or spices.
